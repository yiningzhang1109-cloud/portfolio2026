Yining Zhang

Aging HCI · AI Literacy · Experience Design · Storytelling · Gamification

01

Yining's research centers on Aging HCI, AI Literacy, and Gamification. Around these interests, he has explored interaction design, data visualization, storytelling, game design, and AI-generated content. He believes good design is about helping people from all walks of life embrace technology on their own terms. He is drawn to the meaning technology carries: the AI embedded smartly in a tool, gamified ways of learning and thinking, and designs that linger.

02

Yining studied Media and Communication at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University, and is currently completing an M.S. in Global Media and Culture at Georgia Institute of Technology. He works as a Research Assistant in the TechsAge Lab, contributing to the Tele-TaiChi project, designing for movement, rehabilitation, and social connection among older adults with disabilities.

03

These are some of the things he has made and is currently making: data physicalizations, critical installations, interactive fiction, educational RPGs, speculative design objects, interactive narratives, physical computing installations, AI workflow tools, and Claude skills.
